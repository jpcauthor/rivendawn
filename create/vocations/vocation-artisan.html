Artisan

You are the Artisan — the one who hears the silent song locked inside raw stone, cold iron, and living wood. With patient fire and unyielding will, you coax purpose from chaos and shape things meant to endure.

An Artisan does not merely build. They leave behind works that outlast memory, carrying skill, purpose, and quiet defiance against decay.

Heritage Imprint

◆

The Artisan stands at the meeting point of labor and legacy, turning raw material into something worthy of memory. Whether shaping tools, weapons, or enduring works of craft, this is the vocation of patience, precision, and pride made tangible.

To walk this path is to understand that creation is never accidental. Every mark, measure, and finished piece reflects a mind trained to bring order, usefulness, and beauty into the world.

"Practiced" Mastery Level

Craft was your daily life. The echo remains — you still instinctively know how things fit, break, or endure.

Selectable Skills

Choose two from the four below

Knowledge Engineering

The Artisan's language of structure — load-bearing angles, material interlock, mechanical harmony. Without it, creations collapse or waste effort; with it, you build things that stand for generations.

Ability Weapon Mastery

Many Artisans forged weapons as their highest calling — understanding balance, edge geometry, and how steel sings when swung. Wield your own creations with the same precision you used to shape them.

Knowledge Economics & Trade

Artisans lived or died by trade — material costs, market values, guild politics, what people would pay for beauty or utility. This Knowledge kept workshops alive and ensured your work reached those who needed it.

Ability Perception

A craftsperson's eye misses nothing — the flaw in the grain, the stress fracture forming, the exact moment metal is ready to fold. This sharp attention carries into every aspect of the Artisan's world.
