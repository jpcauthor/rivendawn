Night Watchman

The Nightwatchman chose not to look away when the world preferred shadows. Theirs was the steady vigil no one else wanted — the moonless streets, the whispers in alleys, the slow accumulation of details that others let dissolve into darkness. They learned to see in the dark not just shapes, but intentions, and in doing so became the thing that threats feared most: a witness who remembered.

Imprinted in Social & Perceptual, the Nightwatchman carries into their Calling a practiced mastery of observation and intuition sharpened in dim light — the ability to read hidden agendas, sense deception before it acts, and hold silence as both shield and instrument — skills that prove as lethal in a negotiation as on a midnight patrol.
