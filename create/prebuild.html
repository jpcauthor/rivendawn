Step 12

Liabilities & Boons

Not Started

Take up to 6 Severity Points in Liabilities. Those points become a shared pool — spend them on Boons, convert them to Freebie Points at 1:1, or split between both. Liabilities are permanent and cannot be bought off later.

0 Spent on Boons

Select Liabilities

Enter a severity value (1–6) for each liability you wish to take. Total cannot exceed 6.

Select Boons

Click boons to select them. Cost is deducted from your Liability pool — remainder flows to Freebie Points.

Remember: Freebie Points cannot buy Boons. Boon Points (from Liabilities) cannot be used in the Freebie Point section. Any unspent Liability points after Boons are purchased automatically become Freebie Points.
