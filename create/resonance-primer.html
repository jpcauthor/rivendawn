Resonance

A Primer for Players — What it is, how it works, and how to choose your Path.

What Is Resonance?

Before steel is drawn or the first command of a Calling is spoken, there exists a deeper rhythm beneath the world of Rivendawn — a quiet lattice that scholars, mystics, and Watchers alike call simply the Song. Resonance is the disciplined understanding of that hidden structure.

The Song is neither conscious nor benevolent. It is the underlying order that allows reality to maintain its shape. Rivers follow its rhythm. Storms rise and fall according to its pulse. Even the quiet growth of a seed beneath the soil reflects its deeper cadence.

Those who study Resonance do not summon power from beyond the world. They listen to the harmonics already present within it — and learn, through study and discipline, to issue precise instructions that the Song may accept and carry forward.

Resonance is not conjuration. It is not prayer. It is structured instruction — and the Song obeys, within law.

  A Watcher's Address

The Song is not magic. It is not a gift from gods. It is not a curse placed upon you. It is what remains.

The world hums. It always has. Before your Citadels, before your wars, before you began arguing about who owns truth — the hum was there. It moves through stone, through water, through flesh, through memory. You do not create it. You do not command it. You align with it.

Each Path obeys a First Law. Attempt to bend that Law and the Song resists you — not because it judges, not because it is offended, but because it was never designed to do what you are asking of it.

  • Motion displaces.
  • Flame transforms.
  • Water absorbs.
  • Earth refuses.
  • Growth adapts.
  • Shadow distorts.

These are not suggestions. They are constraints.

The Wellspring

Every Resonant practitioner draws upon an internal reserve of focus and spiritual alignment called the Wellspring. This reservoir represents the mind's capacity to engage with the Song without fracturing under its complexity.

When the Wellspring is depleted, the connection collapses. This is preservation, not punishment. The Wellspring may be restored through rest, through realignment, and through Resonance freely given by others. The Song recognizes voluntary harmony. It does not respond to theft.

Choosing Your Path

Path Points are allocated at character creation and are permanent once assigned. They define your harmonic identity — what the Song recognizes in you, and how it answers when you reach for it.

No bloodline owns the Song. No empire controls it. The difference between peoples is not possession, but attunement. Some lineages resonate more easily with particular Paths. These are affinities — not permissions.

Specialization

Concentrating your Points into one or two Paths increases precision and depth. You become harder to resist within your chosen harmonic — but your options narrow.

Versatility

Spreading your Points across multiple Paths increases flexibility. You can respond to more situations — but with less force in each. Both approaches are lawful. The choice defines identity.

The Six Lawful Paths

Shadowmancy is not a lawful Path and cannot be selected at character creation. See the note below.

Aeris The Path of Displacement
Governs: Agility

Safety through displacement. Advantage through positioning.

Does Well: Speed, momentum redirection, escape, pursuit, spatial disruption, repositioning.

Refuses: Anchoring, fortification, trapping, structural permanence.

Drift toward failure: Recklessness. Avoidance. Abandonment disguised as survival.

Aqua The Path of Absorption
Governs: Vitality

Stability through endurance. Carry strain inward; redistribute rather than shatter.

Does Well: Pressure regulation, burden carrying, sustained resilience, suppression, emotional attunement.

Refuses: Erasure of harm. Direct domination. Immediate annihilation of force.

Drift toward failure: Stagnation. Emotional suppression. Drowning in accumulated burden.

Ignis The Path of Combustive Transformation
Governs: Intelligence

Change through decisive ignition. Ignis ends stalemates through escalation. It burns to reshape.

Does Well: Rapid alteration, catalytic breakthrough, decisive escalation, damage.

Refuses: Preservation. Sustained stillness. Passive endurance.

Drift toward failure: Volatility. Destruction without purpose.

Terra The Path of Refusal
Governs: Strength

Security through immovability. Terra survives by refusing displacement; it meets force and denies motion.

Does Well: Fortification, anchoring, structural reinforcement, concussive force, sustained resistance.

Refuses: Rapid repositioning. Fluid adaptability. Abandoning ground.

Drift toward failure: Rigidity. Obstruction. Inflexible dominance.

Vitae The Path of Adaptive Growth
Governs: Wisdom

Preservation through adaptive correction. Vitae preserves through refinement — it stabilizes and strengthens what lives.

Does Well: Healing, reinforcement, regenerative stabilization, biological correction, restraint.

Refuses: Lethal force. Corruption of life for destruction. Vitae cannot kill.

Drift toward failure: Overgrowth. Control disguised as care.

Obtenebrate The Path of Perceptual Distortion
Governs: Agility

Victory through misdirection. Obtenebrate alters awareness rather than substance — it wins before confrontation begins.

Does Well: Concealment, infiltration, perceptual manipulation, subtle redirection, misdirection.

Refuses: Open dominance. Sustained frontal confrontation.

Drift toward failure: Manipulation without purpose. Deception as identity.

  Shadowmancy — Not a Lawful Path Shadowmancy does not harmonize with the Song. It exploits absence rather than alignment. It extracts without renewal and destabilizes the structures it touches. Shadowmancy cannot be selected at character creation. Those who pursue it do so outside the law of the Paths — and the consequences are structural, not merely social.
How Resonance Resolves

All acts of Resonance are governed by three immutable conditions. Where any one of these fails, the Cast collapses.

I Clarity of Instruction

The Song answers structured will, not vague desire. You must declare specific intent, scope, and target.

II Magnitude of Disruption

The greater the alteration imposed upon the world, the greater the resistance encountered.

III Cost of Sustenance

The Wellspring is finite. No instruction is without burden. Every Cast draws from your reservoir.

The Resolution Formula
Capability = Primary Attribute + Ability or Knowledge + Path Rating
Target Number = Baseline − Capability
Roll 1d20 — Equal to or above Target Number = SUCCESS

Low governing Attributes reduce reliability of Resonant resolution. Build toward your Path's governing Attribute for best results.

Magnitude Tiers (Target Number Modifier)
Standard +0

Single target or contained effect. Localized, immediate, no modifier.

Expanded +3

Multiple targets or structurally significant alteration. Greater resistance and cost.

Mass +9

Battlefield or environment-wide scale. Extreme resistance. Extreme cost.

Magnitude increases risk. It does not increase cost. A Cast is a single resolved expression — multiple discrete outcomes require multiple Casts.

Special Results
Ultimate Success 20

Perfect harmonic resolution. The Cast succeeds regardless of Target Number. Magnitude modifiers still apply to the declared action. A Natural 20 does not expand scope beyond what was declared. Luck does not amplify — it validates.

Harmonic Inversion 1

The declared effect manifests upon the caster instead of the intended target. If the Cast was declared at Expanded or Mass scope, the backlash reflects that magnitude. The Song does not punish. It mirrors.

Limits & Constraints

Resonance rewards knowledge — but instruction must obey law. Combustion requires combustible matter. Pressure requires medium. Mass must exist to be moved. Life cannot be weaponized through Vitae.

The Song does not create impossibility. It accelerates what is already possible.

Living beings also resist deep alteration. Resonance may strike exterior surfaces, constrain movement, agitate surface matter, or reshape environment. Direct lethal alteration of internal structure requires physical interaction or a lawful pathway. The world protects its living patterns.

Harmonic Dissonance
  The Static Veil Harmonic Dissonance occurs when a character's declared Harmonics contradict repeated behavior without narrative transformation. When Dissonance is active, the Static Veil applies: +3 to all Target Numbers for Ability, Knowledge, and Resonant Cast checks. Dissonance increases difficulty — it does not alter Capability or Baseline. It remains until the character demonstrates sustained realignment or formally redefines their Harmonic identity. Application is at Watcher discretion.
