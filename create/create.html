Step 1

Choose Your Name

What You Are Doing: You are grounding your character in a world that is culturally layered and historically deep.

Creative Directive

Avoid "normalized" names. Aim for distinct phonetics, uncommon syllable patterns, and a mythic tone.

Step 6

Determine Primary Ratings

What You Are Doing: You are establishing six foundational capabilities that define what your character's body, mind, and presence can naturally support. These are not skills — they are the bedrock everything else is built upon.

Roll Method

Roll 2d8 → Take the highest → Divide by 2 (Round Down)

e.g., an 8 result equals a Rating of 4

System Cap

Maximum starting rating is 4 / Practiced. This cap applies during and after Character Creation. There are no exceptions.

Forged Combatants: Receive a single FREE Rating Point to spend as they see fit. This free point cannot be used to bypass the creation cap of 4.

The Six Primary Ratings

Roll once for each. Each Primary also serves as a Resonant Anchor — the foundation your Resonant Path draws from.

Agility

Resonant Anchor: Aeris & Obtenebrate

Bodily coordination, precision, and control. Governs dodging, repositioning, weapon attacks relying on timing, fine motor tasks, sleight of hand, and steady aim. Motion and control unified.

1 · Clumsy 2 · Unsteady 3 · Coordinated 4 · Precise ✦ 5 · Fluid 6 · Preternatural

Strength

Resonant Anchor: Terra

Raw physical power and force generation. Governs lifting, breaking, forcing movement, and delivering heavy blows. Commitment made physical.

1 · Weak 2 · Slight 3 · Solid 4 · Powerful ✦ 5 · Mighty 6 · Overwhelming

Vitality

Resonant Anchor: Aqua

Endurance, resilience, and sustained effort. Governs resistance to fatigue, sustained defense, and survival under hardship. Not force — refusal to fail.

1 · Frail 2 · Winded 3 · Resilient 4 · Hardy ✦ 5 · Unyielding 6 · Relentless

Intelligence

Resonant Anchor: Ignis

Structured reasoning, analysis, and formal understanding. Governs investigation, technical knowledge, strategic planning, and logical diagnosis. Understands through analysis.

1 · Limited 2 · Basic 3 · Reasoned 4 · Sharp ✦ 5 · Brilliant 6 · Formidable

Wisdom

Resonant Anchor: Vitae

Experiential awareness, judgment, and intuitive understanding. Governs situational awareness, sensing danger, and recognizing emotional truth. Understands through experience.

1 · Naïve 2 · Uncertain 3 · Sound 4 · Insightful ✦ 5 · Sage 6 · Profound

Social

No Resonant Anchor

Interpersonal awareness and influence. Governs persuasion, deception, intimidation, and shaping outcomes through presence and communication. Influences what others believe and how they respond.

1 · Off-Putting 2 · Uneasy 3 · Personable 4 · Compelling ✦ 5 · Magnetic 6 · Commanding

Step 8

Assign Path Rating & Wellspring

What You Are Doing: You are determining your character's depth of alignment with the Song. (Skip if Forged Combatant.)

Starting Pools

  • Hybrid: 3 pts
  • Pure Resonant: 6 pts
  • Songweaver: 4 pts

The Cap

  • Hybrid cap is 3 (Lineage Affinity does not apply)
  • Others may reach 4 (Mastery)
Defensive Affinity (Resonant Casters): Becomes available if you possess Path Rating 4.

Starting Wellspring

Your starting Wellspring is determined by your Calling.

  • Hybrid Callings — Wellspring Die: d8
  • Pure Resonant Callings — Wellspring Die: d12
  • Songweavers — Wellspring Die: d10

Wellspring Die & Base Wellspring

  • Hybrid d8 — Base Wellspring of 8
  • Pure Resonant d12 — Base Wellspring of 12

Modifier Calculation

  1. Roll 2d(Wellspring Die)
  2. Take highest of the two rolls
  3. Multiply highest roll by 2
  4. Add to Base Wellspring

Hybrid Example

2d8 rolled: 6 | 2 → Highest = 6 × 2 = 12 → Total: 8 + 12 = 20

Pure Resonant Example

2d12 rolled: 11 | 3 → Highest = 11 × 2 = 22 → Total: 12 + 22 = 34

This value becomes your character's starting Wellspring Pool.

Step 9

Determine Defensive Profile

What You Are Doing: You are calculating your structural durability.

HP Formula

24 + (Rolled Health Die × 3)

Health Dice are listed in the Calling Section and multiplied by 3

HP Examples

  • Scribe — 24 + d8 → rolls 6 × 3 = 18 → Total: 24 + 18 = 42
  • Shield Warden — 24 + d12 → rolls 10 × 3 = 30 → Total: 24 + 30 = 54

AP Formula

AP = (Max HP × (Tier × 0.5)), rounded up

AP Examples

  • Scribe — 42 × 0.5 → Armor Points: 21
  • Shield Warden — 52 × 0.5 → Armor Points: 26

Resolution

While AP > 0, damage is split 50/50 between AP and HP.

Step 10

Combat Configuration

What You Are Doing: You are identifying your Offensive Category, distributing Combat Points between Offensive Rating and Base Defensive Rating, and selecting your Defensive Posture.

Offensive Category

Your Calling determines your Offensive Category. This sets your Combat Point pool, your access to Resonance in combat, and your martial ceiling.

Forged Combatant

6 Combat Points

Pure martial discipline. No Wellspring. No Path use. Your power comes entirely from training, precision, and combat instinct.

  • No Wellspring  ·  No Path access
  • +3 Ability & Knowledge Points
  • +1 additional Primary Attribute point
  • +5 additional Freebie Points

Martial Hybrid

5 Combat Points

Weapon combat fused with limited Resonance. You operate in both worlds — but neither fully.

  • Path Rating cap: 3 per Path
  • True Resonant Caster cap: 4
  • May not access Expanded or Mass magnitude
  • High power  ·  High resource volatility

Forged Combatant (6 pts) / Hybrid (5 pts)

Distribute between Offensive Rating (OR) and Base Defensive Rating (BDR)

  • Minimum: 1  |  Maximum: 4
  • Rogue (Forged Combatant): 3 pts OR / 2 pts BDR
  • Hybrid Ranger: 3 pts OR / 2 pts BDR

Defensive Rating + Defensive Posture

Base Defensive Rating + Defensive Posture = Total Defensive Rating

  • Dodge: Gain +2 to Base Defensive Rating
  • Parry: If opponent misses, reduce your Target Number (TN) by 2 on your next attack against them
  • Riposte: The attacker reduces their Target Number (TN) by 1

Pure Resonant

4 Combat Points

The Song is your weapon. Martial combat is a fallback, not a foundation. Full access to Expanded and Mass magnitude.

  • Offensive Rating cap at creation: 2
  • Path Rating cap: 4
  • Full access to Expanded and Mass magnitude
  • Unspent Combat Points convert — player's choice — to Resonance resources

Pure Resonant (4 pts)

Distribute between Offensive Rating (OR) and Base Defensive Rating (BDR). Offensive Rating caps at 2 at creation.

  • Any unspent Combat Points convert — player's choice — to Resonance resources:

Conversion Options

2 unspent points → +1 Path Point

1 unspent point  → +5 Wellspring Capacity

Unspent points may be split across both options. Example: 2 unspent → +1 Path Point, or +10 Wellspring, or 1 Path Point + 5 Wellspring.

Valid Pure Resonant builds: 2 OR / 2 BDR (no conversion)  ·  1 OR / 2 BDR (1 pt converts)  ·  1 OR / 1 BDR (2 pts convert)  ·  2 OR / 1 BDR (1 pt converts)

Pure Resonant Defensive Postures

  • Absorption Shield (Aqua or Terra)
    • Roll a number of d4 equal to your Path Rating
    • Add the result to your Armor Points as Bonus Armor
    • Bonus Armor: Functions like normal Armor; reduced first when Armor damage is applied; does not prevent Health damage from the 50/50 rule
  • Damage Shield (Ignis)
    • Provides no avoidance bonus
    • When struck in melee, the attacker takes retaliatory Ignis damage
    • Damage determined using the Resonant Dice table
  • Dodge (Aeris)
    • Adds +2 to Total Defensive Rating
    • Provides no Damage Shield or Absorption Shield
Step 11

Determine Initiative

What You Are Doing: You are establishing your combat tempo.

  • Forged / Hybrid: Primary Rating used for Combat (Str or Agi) + d20
  • Pure Resonant: Highest Path Rating + d20
  • Example: A Physicker has a Vitae of 4. They roll a d20 and get 14. Their initiative for that round is 18.

Step 14

Resources & Finances

What You Are Doing: You are establishing your character's starting foundation in the economy and social strata.

The Golden Rule: These are purchased exclusively with Freebie Points (FP) and cannot be subsidized by Liabilities. See Step 13 for FP costs.

Mechanical Impact

Finances: Establish starting capital

  • Rank 1 — 100 MK
  • Rank 2 — 500 MK
  • Rank 3 — 2,500 MK

Social Resources: Purchase advantages like Informant Networks, Benefactors, or Legal Immunity.

System Cap

No category may exceed Rank 3 at creation.
