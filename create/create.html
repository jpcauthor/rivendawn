Choose Your Name

Step 3

Choose Your Harmonics

You are declaring your internal alignment — how you resonate with the world morally and philosophically.

Harmonics establishes your Dissonance Triggers and Static Veil penalties. It does not modify combat math or attributes. Your identity is formed from a Dynamic (the pulse of your power) paired with a Discipline (the shape of your action).

  • What principle do you refuse to betray?
  • Where are you flexible — and where does the Song notice when you aren't?

Step 6

Combat Configuration

You are identifying your Offensive Category, distributing Combat Points between Offensive Rating and Base Defensive Rating, and selecting your Defensive Posture.

Forged Combatant

6 Combat Points

Pure martial discipline. No Wellspring. No Path use. Power comes from training, precision, and instinct.

  • No Wellspring  ·  No Path access
  • +3 Ability & Knowledge Points
  • +1 free Primary Rating point
  • +5 additional Freebie Points

Resonant Hybrid

5 Combat Points

Weapon combat fused with limited Resonance. You operate in both worlds — but neither fully.

  • Path Rating cap: 3 per Path
  • May not access Expanded or Mass magnitude
  • Wellspring Die: d8

True Resonant Caster

4 Combat Points

The Song is your weapon. Martial combat is a fallback. Full access to Expanded and Mass magnitude.

  • Offensive Rating cap at creation: 2
  • Path Rating cap: 4  ·  Wellspring Die: d12
  • Unspent Combat Points convert to Resonance resources: 2 pts → +1 Path Point  |  1 pt → +5 Wellspring

Distributing Combat Points

Distribute between Offensive Rating (OR) and Base Defensive Rating (BDR). Minimum 1 · Maximum 4 per stat.

Defensive Postures — Forged & Hybrid

  • Dodge: +2 to Base Defensive Rating
  • Parry: On opponent miss, reduce your TN by 2 on your next attack against them
  • Riposte: The attacker reduces their TN by 1

Defensive Postures — Pure Resonant

  • Absorption Shield (Aqua or Terra): Roll d4s equal to Path Rating → add result to Armor Points as Bonus Armor
  • Damage Shield (Ignis): When struck in melee, attacker takes retaliatory Ignis damage
  • Dodge (Aeris): +2 to Total Defensive Rating

Step 10

Defensive Profile

You are calculating your structural durability.

HP Formula

24 + (Health Die Roll × 3)

Health Die is listed on your Calling page.

AP Formula

HP × (0.Tier), rounded up

Tier 1 = × 0.1  ·  Tier 5 = × 0.5  ·  Tier 9 = × 0.9. AP can never exceed HP at creation.

50/50 Rule: While AP > 0, all incoming damage is split evenly between AP and HP.
Step 11

Determine Initiative

You are establishing your combat tempo. Initiative is rolled fresh each combat round.

  • Forged / Hybrid: Primary Rating used for Combat (Str or Agi) + d20
  • Pure Resonant: Highest Path Rating + d20
  • Example: A Physicker with Vitae 4 rolls a d20 and gets 14 — Initiative is 18.
