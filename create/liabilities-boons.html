Liabilities & Boons

The Weight of a Life
No soul walks the Song untouched. Every life in Rivendawn is shaped by pressures both visible and unseen — history, circumstance, memory, blessing, loss. Liabilities are the burdens that complicate your every step. Boons are the gifts that set you apart. Together, they define not just what your character can do — but what they must overcome, and why their story matters.

How It Works

The Cap You may take up to 6 Severity Points of Liabilities total. This is an absolute cap.
The Conversion Each Liability Point converts 1:1 into Boon Points. 6 Liability Points = 6 Boon Points.
Spend or Lose Boon Points must be spent during character creation. Unspent points are lost when creation ends.
Permanent Once creation is complete, selections are fixed. Liabilities cannot be bought off mechanically — only resolved through story.
The Watcher's Note: The Watcher is a storyteller. When you hand them the ammunition to complicate your group's life — that part is on you. Choose carefully. And choose boldly.
