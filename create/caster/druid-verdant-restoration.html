Druid — Verdant Restoration

The Keeper of Renewal

✦
The Verdant Restoration embodies the quiet resilience of the living world. Forests burn and return. Rivers flood and then calm. Wounds close, bark regrows, and life pushes upward through broken stone. The Verdant Restoration channels that same ancient cycle. They do not merely mend wounds. They restore balance. Where devastation has passed, the Restoration ensures life does not fade. The wild does not simply survive. It heals.

Identity — Life's Persistence

The Verdant Restoration studies the natural cycles that sustain the world — regeneration, renewal after destruction, ecological balance, and the persistence of life under pressure. They understand that healing is not weakness. It is the stubborn refusal of life to be extinguished. Where the Restoration stands, collapse becomes temporary.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Surge of Renewal

Trigger: When an opponent rolls an Ultimate Failure against the Restoration.

Life energy surges through the battlefield. The Restoration or an ally within 6 meters restores 1d6 HP.

The attacker's failure releases a burst of restorative Resonance, like fresh rain feeding the soil.

Offensive Gift

Blooming Reversal

Trigger: When the Restoration rolls an Ultimate Success.

The attack channels life energy through the strike. The target suffers +1d6 additional damage. An ally within 6 meters restores 1d6 HP.

As the Resonant energy strikes the enemy, life force blooms outward, flowing back into an ally like sunlight breaking through forest canopy.

Signature Surges

Offensive Surge

Verdant Overflow

The Restoration converts destructive energy into restorative life force.

Trigger — Declare before making a Resonant attack

The attack deals normal damage. An ally within 8 meters restores HP equal to half the damage dealt.

The attack releases a surge of living energy that spills outward, feeding nearby life even as it strikes the enemy.

Defensive Surge

Cycle of Renewal

The Restoration invokes the deep regenerative cycle of nature to prevent catastrophic loss. May be declared after an ally within range has been targeted — cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When an ally within 8 meters takes damage

Reduce the incoming damage by 50% and restore 1d6 HP.

Life answers violence with renewal. The wound closes even as the strike lands, the forest's resilience flowing through the Restoration's power.

Weapon Affinity

Staff Wand Orb Druidic Focus

These implements channel restorative Resonance rather than serving as conventional weapons.

Playstyle Hook

You are the breath after the storm. Where the Cataclysm tears the battlefield apart, you ensure that life continues when the violence ends. Enemies may wound your allies. They may burn the forest and scar the land. But life has endured worse. And through you, it rises again.
