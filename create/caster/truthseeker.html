Truthseeker

The Hound

✦
The Truthseeker is the Hound. They do not guess. They track. Pattern is proof; deviation is confession. What is hidden will be dragged into the high-contrast light of certainty. Lies leave residue. Illusions distort symmetry. Concealment creates tension in reality itself. The Truthseeker follows those fractures until concealment collapses.

Identity — Pattern Recognition as Weapon

The Truthseeker studies deviation, behavioral inconsistency, shadow displacement, and environmental irregularity. They understand that deception requires maintenance. Through Resonant calibration, they amplify contrast until what is hidden cannot remain so. The battlefield is not just terrain; it is evidence.

Defensive Postures

Resonant Casters — Unlocked at Creation

Absorption Shield

Aqua or Terra. Increase attacker's TN by 1. Roll d4 equal to Path Rating and add total to Armor Points as Bonus Armor.

Damage Shield

Ignis. No avoidance bonus. When struck in melee, the attacker takes retaliatory Ignis damage.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Certainty Barrier

Once per long rest, the Truthseeker may invoke a Resonant deflection shield that grants +2 to Defensive Rating for the duration of a combat encounter. This shield stacks with other Resonant defenses. When certainty locks in, hesitation vanishes.

Offensive Gift

Veilbreaker Instinct

The Truthseeker's damaging abilities strike hidden, stealthed, or concealed targets as though they were fully revealed. Illusion, concealment, and shadow do not negate their precision. If it exists within pattern, it can be struck.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Revealed Sequence

The Truthseeker follows the chain of motion to its inevitable conclusion.

Trigger — After successfully resolving an attack

The Truthseeker may immediately make a second attack against the same target.
• This attack cannot trigger Gifts or Surges.
• This attack cannot chain into additional attacks.

The first strike reveals the sequence. The second completes it.

Defensive Surge

Pattern Slip

By recognizing the structure of the attack, the Truthseeker slips through the weakest point. May be declared after taking damage — cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When taking damage

Reduce incoming damage by 40%.

The strike lands where the Truthseeker no longer stands.

Weapon Affinity

Playstyle Hook

You hunt lies. You read tension in the air and fracture concealment through Resonant contrast. Hidden foes are not safe. Illusions are temporary. When you commit to pursuit, evasion becomes delay — not escape.
