Freebie Points

Final Refinement
After the broad strokes of your history have been painted — once your Vocation has been named and your Heritage Imprints have found their rhythm — there remains a final moment of sharpening. Freebie Points are not a new identity. They are the extra miles you walked, the private hours of practice others overlooked. The final polish on the blade. The deepening of a well-worn path.

Economic Separation: Freebie Points and the Liabilities & Boons system are completely separate. Liabilities may not generate or convert into Freebie Points. Freebie Points may not be used to purchase Boons. These systems do not interact.

Permitted Expenditures

Freebie Points may only be spent in the following categories. All increases must respect established creation caps.

Category Cost Notes & Caps
Primary Rating 8 FP Raise one Primary Rating by 1. Creation cap: 4. Cannot exceed hard cap under any circumstance.
Ability or Knowledge 2 FP Raise one Ability or Knowledge by 1. Creation cap: 4 after all bonuses applied.
Finances Rank 2 FP Purchase one rank of Finances. Maximum Rank 3 at creation. See Resources & Finances.
Social & Material Resources 2 FP Purchase one rank of Social or Material Resources. Maximum Rank 3 at creation. Freebie Points are the only currency for this.
Resonance Path Point 10 FP Purchase one additional Path Point for Resonance allocation. Must respect Path Rating caps. Hybrids may not exceed 3 per Path via any method.

Example Spend

A Hybrid Ranger — 10 Freebie Points

Raises Agility by 1 8 FP — 2 points remaining.

Raises Stealth (Ability) by 1 2 FP — 0 points remaining.

All points spent. Character sheet locked.

A Forged Combatant — 15 Freebie Points

Raises Strength by 1 8 FP — 7 points remaining.

Raises Weapon Mastery (Ability) by 1 2 FP — 5 points remaining.

Purchases Finances Rank 1 2 FP — 3 points remaining.

Raises Athletics (Ability) by 1 2 FP — 1 point remaining.

1 point unspent — lost at end of creation.

Order of Application

  • 1 Complete all earlier creation steps — Lineage, Vocation, Calling, Primary Ratings, Abilities & Knowledges, Path Allocation, Wellspring.
  • 2 Finalize your Liabilities & Boons selections completely. These are locked before Freebie Points are spent.
  • 3 Spend your Freebie Points. Apply all increases. Verify nothing exceeds the creation cap of 4.
  • 4 Unspent points are lost. They do not carry forward into play. Spend them or forfeit them.
  • 5 Character sheet is locked. Freebie Points cannot be refunded or reallocated without Watcher approval.
The Golden Rule: Freebie Points are the only currency available to purchase ranks of Resources and Finances. They cannot be subsidized by Liabilities, earned through Boons, or generated by any other means. Plan accordingly.
