Shield Warden

The Immovable Object

✦

The Shield Warden is the unbreakable bulwark, the line that does not yield, the wall that refuses collapse. Where others maneuver, the Warden anchors. Where others chase momentum, the Warden steals it.

They do not evade force — they absorb it, redirect it, and return it with disciplined brutality. Sieges break against them. Charges die in front of them. The Shield Warden is endurance made doctrine.

Soul of the Calling

The Shield Warden is the unbreakable bulwark, the line that does not yield, the wall that refuses collapse. Where others maneuver, the Warden anchors. Where others chase momentum, the Warden steals it. They do not evade force — they absorb it, redirect it, and return it with disciplined brutality. Sieges break against them. Charges die in front of them. The Shield Warden is endurance made doctrine.

Identity — Momentum Denial

The Shield Warden studies formation stability, impact angles, line integrity, and pressure transfer. They understand that momentum is a resource — every charge carries force that can be stolen, every strike exposes leverage. The Warden does not simply withstand an attack; they convert it into positional advantage. They are not reactive defenders. They are territorial sovereigns.

Shield Techniques

ActionShield Bash

Incorporate a shield strike into a successful melee attack. Standard Attack Roll. Damage: 1d8.

ActionResonant Shield Mimic

Must declare prior to attack. When the shield strikes, force arcs to two additional targets within 20 feet. Bounce 1: 1d6 / Bounce 2: 1d4. Usable twice per round.

MarkResonant Challenge

Enemies struck by the shield or Mimic increase their TN by 3 to attack anyone but the Shield Warden until the end of their next turn. Usable twice per round.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Retributive Anchor

When an attacker rolls an Ultimate Failure (natural 1) against the Shield Warden, the Warden gains a free Attack of Opportunity using Shield Bash.

Failure against the wall is not free. It is an invitation.

Offensive Gift

Entrenched Momentum

When the Shield Warden rolls an Ultimate Success, their Defensive Rating increases by 3 for three rounds.

Victory does not relax the Warden. It entrenches them.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Crushing Reversal

The Shield Warden steals an attacker's momentum and turns it into immediate punishment.

Trigger — After an enemy misses the Shield Warden with a melee attack

Immediately perform a Shield Bash attack. If the attack hits, choose one:

  • The target is knocked Prone, OR
  • The Shield Bash deals +50% damage.

The attacker's force collapses against the Warden's shield. In the same motion, the Warden drives forward with crushing retaliation.

Defensive Surge

Bulwark of the Line

The Shield Warden extends their defensive dominance to protect those behind the line. May be declared after an ally within 3 meters has been targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When an ally within 3 meters is targeted by an attack

Interpose yourself, becoming the new target of the attack. Reduce the incoming damage by 60%.

The Warden steps forward, shield interposed. The attack crashes against a wall of steel instead of the intended target.

Weapon Affinity

One-Handed Sword + Shield Mace + Shield Hammer Polearm

These weapons complement control, spacing, and territorial dominance rather than speed or finesse.

Playstyle Hook

You are the point where advance collapses. You occupy space, deny passage, and punish overextension. Enemies cannot bypass you without cost. Allies stabilize behind you. You do not chase the battle. You force the battle to break against you.
