Soul of the Calling

The Dawn Sentinel stands where ruin would otherwise advance. They are not merely warriors, but declarations — visible refusals to let darkness consume what cannot defend itself. Their presence is both warning and promise. Where corruption spreads, the Sentinel draws a boundary and holds it. Where allies falter, the Sentinel interposes. They are vigilance embodied, conviction given steel, and light that refuses extinction.

Identity — Radiant Interposition

The Sentinel studies threat priority, ally positioning, moral resolve, and battlefield discipline. They understand that protection is proactive — it requires awareness of who must be shielded and when to step into harm's path. The Dawn Sentinel does not merely fight enemies; they deny enemies access to what they seek to destroy.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Intercessor's Burden

If a groupmate within 10 feet is struck by an Ultimate Success, the Dawn Sentinel may absorb up to 50% of the damage that would be dealt to that groupmate.

This represents deliberate interposition — shield, weapon, or body placed between harm and the innocent. The Sentinel chooses to share the burden.

Offensive Gift

Radiant Concussion

When the Dawn Sentinel scores an Ultimate Success, roll 1d4.

  • Odd result: Target is stunned for 1 round.
  • Even result: Target is stunned for 2 rounds.

This reflects overwhelming, conviction-driven impact that disrupts the enemy's capacity to act.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Radiant Judgment

The Dawn Sentinel delivers a conviction-driven strike meant to halt the enemy's advance.

Trigger — Declare before making an attack

  • Gain +4 Offensive Rating for the attack.
  • If the attack hits, it deals +50% damage.

The Sentinel's weapon falls with unwavering certainty, the strike carrying the weight of a sworn refusal to yield.

Defensive Surge

Light That Endures

The Dawn Sentinel refuses collapse, drawing upon conviction and resolve to remain standing when others depend on them. May be declared after taking damage — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — After taking damage

Immediately restore 25% of the damage just taken.

The Sentinel steadies themselves beneath the blow, drawing strength from conviction and the knowledge that the line must hold.

Weapon Affinity

Playstyle Hook

You are the visible line between devastation and survival. You absorb danger meant for others and answer aggression with controlled force. Allies endure because you endure first. You do not simply fight darkness — you stand where it must stop.
