Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Offensive Gift

Echoed Cadence

On an Ultimate Success with an Expression, immediately repeat the same Expression against a second valid target within range.

  • No additional WP cost
  • Cannot trigger additional Gifts
  • Does not chain

Defensive Gift

Harmonic Backlash

When an opponent rolls an Ultimate Failure against you, immediately apply a Minor Cadence effect of your choice to that opponent.

  • No WP cost
  • Cannot escalate beyond Minor

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Perfect Echo

The Songweaver folds the Cadence back upon itself, producing a second identical expression before the rhythm dissipates.

Trigger — After successfully resolving a Cadence Expression

Repeat the same Expression against the same target.

  • No additional WP cost
  • Cannot trigger Gifts
  • Second result uses the same Band as the original

The note reverberates through the structure of the moment. Before the rhythm fades, the Cadence strikes again.

Defensive Surge

Harmonic Shield

The Songweaver bends the Cadence around themselves to disperse incoming force. May be declared after being targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When taking damage

Reduce incoming damage by 40%.

The strike lands against a shifting harmonic field. The impact disperses through layered resonance rather than flesh.

Cadence Effect Resolution

1. Roll Expression vs Defensive Rating or Object DR.

2. On success, determine Band by Cadence Rating.

3. Apply effect from the appropriate Cadence table.

4. If Dominant, DC 12 may apply.

5. WP cost is paid on attempt.

Playstyle Hook

You do not strike with steel or flame — you reshape the rhythm of the moment itself. You extend what helps, disrupt what threatens, suspend what must not move. The Cadence answers those who listen closely enough to keep pace with it. You are not the loudest voice in the room. You are the one that changes what every other voice does.
