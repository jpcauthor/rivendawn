The Living Bastion

✦

The Verdant Thornheart is the will of the wild given form. They do not plead for nature's survival — they enforce it. Stone hardens beneath their step. Roots twist through the soil. The living world does not beg for mercy; it grows teeth.

The Thornheart is both protector and retaliation. Like the thorns of a rose or the bark of an ancient oak, their defense carries consequence. They are the wall grown from the earth itself.

Soul of the Calling

The Verdant Thornheart is the will of the wild given form. They do not plead for nature's survival — they enforce it. Where forests are cut, rivers poisoned, and beasts driven from their rightful ground, the Thornheart answers with uncompromising presence. Stone hardens beneath their step. Roots twist through the soil. The living world does not beg for mercy; it grows teeth. Where the Verdant Restoration heals the living world and the Verdant Cataclysm unleashes its fury, the Thornheart stands between destruction and what must survive. They are the wall grown from the earth itself.

Identity — Guardian of the Living Ground

The Thornheart studies terrain, root systems, soil stability, and natural pressure points within the land itself. They understand that nature survives not through avoidance but through endurance. When the Thornheart takes their stance, the battlefield changes. Their footing deepens. Their blows carry the weight of earth and petrified wood. Enemies who attempt to overwhelm them discover the truth of the wild: the forest may bend — but its roots hold.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Offensive Gift

Petrified Stone Strike

When the Thornheart rolls an Ultimate Success, the strike lands with crushing geological force. The attack deals +1d8 additional damage.

The blow lands with the weight of ancient earth behind it. The impact feels less like steel and more like being struck by a petrified log — dense, immovable, and unforgiving.

Defensive Gift

Grasp of the Wild

When an opponent rolls a natural 1 against the Thornheart, nature punishes the mistake. Roll 1d4:

  • Odd: The attacker's next attack increases by +4 TN.
  • Even: The attacker becomes Restrained for 1 round as the ground grips their footing.

The earth shifts beneath the attacker's feet. Soil grips, roots twist, and the wild itself punishes reckless aggression.

Signature Surges

Offensive Surge

Cataclysmic Blow

The Thornheart channels the violent strength of the earth into a devastating strike.

Trigger — Declare before making a melee attack

  • Gain +4 Offensive Rating for the attack.
  • If the attack hits, it deals +50% damage.

The Thornheart's weapon descends like a falling tree, the impact carrying the crushing force of the land itself.

Defensive Surge

Barkskin Aegis

The Thornheart hardens their body with living resilience, absorbing an otherwise devastating blow. May be declared after being targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When taking damage

Reduce incoming damage by 50%.

A hardened layer of bark-like resilience spreads across the Thornheart's body, the strike dispersing through living structure rather than flesh.

Weapon Affinity

Quarterstaff Greatclub Polearm Stone-bound Shield

These weapons favor leverage, rooted stance, and crushing power over speed or finesse.

Playstyle Hook

You are the wild's refusal to collapse. Enemies may burn the forest and scar the land, but when they push too far they find something waiting — immovable, patient, and unforgiving. The Thornheart does not chase destruction. They stand where it must stop.
