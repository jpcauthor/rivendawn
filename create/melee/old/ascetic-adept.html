Ascetic Adept

The Living Conduit

✦

The Ascetic Adept refines flesh into instrument and breath into discipline. They do not overwhelm — they dismantle. Each strike is calibrated. Each motion deliberate. Their power is inevitability applied through mastery.

Where others rely on armor or raw force, the Adept controls tempo, disrupts rhythm, and wins exchanges through precision and positioning. They are the still point at the center of the storm.

Soul of the Calling

Identity — The Living Conduit

The Adept controls tempo in combat, wins exchanges through discipline and positioning, uses moment-based defense rather than constant avoidance, and disrupts opponents rather than overpowering them. No passive dodge inflation. No immunity mechanics. No forced Dual Wield. No parallel defensive engine.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Offensive Gift

Conduit Disruption

On an Ultimate Success:

  • If the target is armed → they are Disarmed.
  • If unarmed → The Adept's next attack decreases by +3 TN.

Triggers once per Ultimate Success event. The Adept breaks rhythm, not bone.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Meridian Break

The Adept attacks the structural weak points of the body, disrupting breath, nerve response, and muscular stability.

Trigger — After successfully hitting a target

Choose one:

  • The attack deals +50% damage, OR
  • The target's next attack increases by +4 TN.

A precise strike lands against a vital point — a nerve cluster, breath channel, or structural joint. The opponent's rhythm collapses instantly.

Defensive Surge

Iron Breath

The Adept absorbs punishment through perfect internal control. May be declared after being targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When taking damage

Reduce incoming damage by 50%.

The strike lands — but breath, posture, and tension redirect the force through the body rather than against it.
