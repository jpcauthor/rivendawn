Identity — Swift Adjudication

The Wayfarer Knight studies distance, approach vectors, mounted and dismounted engagement, and the psychology of confrontation. They understand that speed resolves escalation before it spreads. A duel is not chaos; it is containment. The Knight isolates threat, controls tempo, and concludes conflict before collateral damage blooms. Their Resonance sharpens motion rather than expands spectacle.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Interceding Momentum

Once per combat encounter, the Wayfarer Knight may move to intercede for a target within 10 feet. They may apply their Combat Rating to the attacker's roll.

If the protected target has a higher Defensive Rating, the Knight may instead absorb the full attack. This represents decisive intervention and controlled displacement of threat.

Offensive Gift

Adjudicated Strike

On an Ultimate Success, the Wayfarer Knight may either disarm or subdue an opponent with a precise strike. Roll 1d4:

  • Odd result: Effect lasts 1 round.
  • Even result: Effect lasts 2 rounds.

The strike is not reckless — it is corrective.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Blade of Judgment

The Wayfarer Knight isolates the opponent and strips them of advantage through precise dueling technique.

Trigger — After successfully hitting an opponent with a melee attack

Choose one:

  • The target is Disarmed, OR
  • The target's next attack increases by +4 TN.

Steel turns with surgical precision. The opponent's weapon is knocked loose or their stance collapses under the Knight's exacting strike.

Defensive Surge

Roadwarden's Guard

The Wayfarer Knight protects the vulnerable by placing themselves between threat and victim. May be declared after an ally within 3 meters has been targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When an ally within 3 meters is targeted by an attack

Interpose yourself, becoming the new target of the attack. Reduce the incoming damage by 40%.

The Knight moves instantly, steel and cloak cutting between attacker and ally. The blow meant for another lands instead against the roadwarden.

Playstyle Hook

You do not wait for injustice to spread. You arrive first, isolate the threat, and resolve it at blade's reach. You move quickly, intercede decisively, and end conflicts before they metastasize. The road remains safe because you ride it.
