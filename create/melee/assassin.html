Identity — Precision Without Witness

The Assassin studies sight lines, escape vectors, reaction windows, breath cycles, and structural vulnerabilities. They do not attack randomly — they attack when resistance is weakest and outcome is irreversible. The difference between murder and execution is preparation. The Assassin prepares. Their Resonance is not theatrical. It reinforces timing, edge control, and inevitability. Their power is not visible. It is terminal.

Skill Matrix

AbilityStealth

Control over visibility — movement discipline, environmental awareness, and silence under stress. An Assassin who is seen too early has already failed.

AbilityWeapon Mastery

Every weapon is an extension of precision — angles, grip transitions, draw timing, and release control. Whether blade, bow, or crossbow, minimal motion yields maximal effect.

AbilityReaction Timing

Assassins operate inside fractions of a second — exploiting flinches, delayed guards, and overextensions to strike first or disengage cleanly.

KnowledgeMedicine

An Assassin does not guess where to strike. They understand arteries, joint failure points, nerve clusters, and shock thresholds. When they hit, systems fail.

Defensive Postures

Dodge

Gain +2 to Base Defensive Rating.

Parry

Riposte

Attacker reduces their TN by 1; if they miss, gain a full attack of opportunity.

Ranged / Sniper Builds — Unlocked at Creation

Evade / Cover

Attacker suffers +2 TN when target benefits from cover against a ranged attack.

Braced

No defensive bonus; if attacker misses, return fire gains +1 success.

Steady Aim

No defensive bonus; if attacker misses, return fire gains +3 success. Must remain stationary.

Bestowed Gifts of the Calling

Defensive Gift

Elusive Predator

The Assassin is immune to normal Attacks of Opportunity. An Attack of Opportunity may only occur if the Assassin is slowed, restrained, or otherwise impaired.

This reflects superior positioning, escape discipline, and situational awareness. Defensive survival is achieved through training, posture, and positioning — not Resonant shields or absorption.

Offensive Gift

Surgical Breach

On an Ultimate Success, the Assassin's damage bypasses Armor Points. If the target has no remaining Armor Points, roll an additional 1d4 damage and add it to the total.

This represents striking through seams, under plates, between ribs, or at the precise moment armor fails to matter. The Assassin does not overpower defense — they circumvent it.

Combat Surges

Offensive Surge

Killing Window

The Assassin exploits the fleeting instant where defense collapses and outcome becomes inevitable.

Trigger — Declare before making an attack

  • Gain +4 Offensive Rating for the attack.
  • If the attack hits, it deals +50% damage.

The Assassin moves in the instant between guard and recovery. The strike lands before resistance can reform.

Defensive Surge

Predator's Escape

The Assassin abandons the engagement before retaliation stabilizes. May be declared after being targeted — but cannot be used while stunned, disoriented, unconscious, or surprised.

Trigger — When targeted by an attack

Immediately move up to 5 meters. If the attacker cannot reach your new position, the attack automatically misses.

The Assassin slips beyond the attack line in a heartbeat, leaving the strike to cut through empty air.

Playstyle Hook

You eliminate threats before they become battles. You position with intent, strike with finality, and withdraw before reprisal stabilizes. Your strength lies not in endurance but in inevitability. When you act, resistance collapses.
