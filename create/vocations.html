Vocations

Before the Song ever called you to greatness, you were someone ordinary — and that matters. Vocations represent the lives characters lived before destiny found them: the crafts they practiced, the duties they carried, and the quiet skills that shaped who they became. From bakers who fed their towns to scouts who walked lonely trails, these humble beginnings leave lasting marks on every hero. A Vocation grants practical knowledge, instinctive abilities, and a glimpse into the life that forged you before the adventure began.

Vocation Artisan Skilled makers who shape metal, wood, and stone into tools and treasures meant to endure.
Vocation Baker Masters of hearth and oven who turn simple ingredients into the daily bread of communities.
Vocation Caretaker Compassionate guardians of the sick and weary who mend bodies and calm troubled spirits.
Vocation Caretaker (Advanced) Experienced healers whose practiced hands and steady presence keep others alive through hardship.
Vocation Courier Swift runners and roadwise travelers trusted to deliver vital messages across dangerous lands.
Vocation Hearthwarden Watchful protectors who keep homes safe, fires burning, and families secure through long nights.
Vocation Initiate Resonant Students of the Song who have begun to sense the quiet echoes of Resonance.
Vocation Laborer Builders of roads, walls, and foundations whose strength shapes the physical world.
Vocation Lorekeeper Guardians of memory who preserve history, language, and the stories of Rivendawn.
Vocation Manservant Attentive household aides whose quiet presence reveals more than most ever notice.
Vocation Mediator Voices of reason who step between rivals and turn conflict toward uneasy peace.
Vocation Nightwatchman Silent guardians of the dark hours who notice the small movements others miss.
Vocation Preserver Experts in storage and rationing who keep communities alive through lean seasons.
Vocation Protector Loyal defenders who place themselves between danger and those who cannot fight.
Vocation Scout Silent pathfinders who move ahead of others to reveal the dangers of the wild.
Vocation Steward Organizers of land, coin, and labor who keep estates and households functioning.
Vocation Student Curious minds driven to understand the deeper knowledge hidden within the world.
Vocation Waykeeper Maintainers of roads and passes who keep travel possible across a dangerous world.
