Primary Ratings

The Foundations of Capability

◆

Every character in Rivendawn possesses fundamental capabilities that shape how they interact with the world. These capabilities are known as Primary Ratings.

Primary Ratings represent the natural foundations of a character's body, mind, and presence. They describe what a person is inherently capable of supporting before training, specialization, or Resonant power refines those abilities further.

Where skills represent learned techniques, Primary Ratings describe the deeper structure that makes those techniques possible. In moments of pressure — when danger, discovery, or decisive action defines the course of events — Primary Ratings reveal the limits and potential of a character's capabilities.

They are the core measurements of what a person can reliably accomplish when the Song is in motion.

How Primary Ratings Work in Play

Primary Ratings serve as your foundational input in the Core Resolution Engine.

Capability = Primary Rating + Ability or Knowledge + Context Rating

The hard cap of 4 applies at creation and remains in place. There are no exceptions.

The Six-Step Scale

All ratings use a 1–6 scale. Each point represents a meaningful increase in competence — not a marginal statistical bump.

RatingLabelWhat It Means
1MinimalMinimal training or instinctive capability.
2BasicFamiliarity or limited experience.
3SolidDependable competence.
4Practiced ✦ CapConfident, reliable execution. Maximum at creation — no exceptions.
5ExceptionalRare capability beyond the ordinary.
6MasteryUncommon even among heroes.

Primary Definitions & Ladders

Agility Aeris & Obtenebrate

Resonant Anchor: Aeris & Obtenebrate

Agility measures total bodily coordination, precision, and control. It reflects how naturally a character moves through space and how accurately they execute physical action. Agility governs balance, footwork, posture, breath alignment, and the seamless transition between motion and stillness.

Agility governs dodging, repositioning, acrobatic movement, and all weapon attacks that rely on timing, alignment, and spatial precision. It also governs fine motor control: delicate manipulation, sleight of hand, lockpicking, trap disarming, steady aim, and any task requiring controlled physical exactness.

Aeris expresses Agility as open motion, displacement, and instinctive flow. Obtenebrate expresses Agility as concealed precision, misdirection, and controlled subtlety.

Agility is not brute force and it is not endurance. It is execution — motion and control unified.

Ladder — Increasing control over physical execution
1ClumsyFrequently misaligned or inaccurate.
2UnsteadyBasic control that falters under pressure.
3CoordinatedReliable movement in ordinary conditions.
4 ✦PreciseConsistent accuracy in demanding situations.
5FluidEffortless, efficient execution.
6PreternaturalSupernaturally exact control.
Strength Terra

Resonant Anchor: Terra

Strength measures raw physical power and force generation. It reflects a character's ability to apply mass, leverage, and momentum to overcome resistance. Strength governs lifting, breaking, forcing movement, and delivering heavy blows.

Strength is commitment made physical.

Ladder — Increasing reliable force output
1WeakStruggles with significant physical strain.
2SlightLimited force capability.
3SolidReliable physical effort.
4 ✦PowerfulStrong and dependable under strain.
5MightyExceptional force beyond the ordinary.
6OverwhelmingForce that reshapes resistance.
Vitality Aqua

Resonant Anchor: Aqua

Vitality represents endurance, resilience, and sustained effort. It reflects a character's ability to absorb strain, maintain posture, and continue functioning under prolonged stress. Vitality governs resistance to fatigue, sustained defense, and survival under hardship.

Vitality is not force; it is refusal to fail.

Ladder — Increasing resilience and endurance
1FrailEasily exhausted or overwhelmed.
2WindedCan endure briefly before fatigue sets in.
3ResilientMaintains performance through moderate strain.
4 ✦HardyEndures prolonged stress reliably.
5UnyieldingRarely falters under pressure.
6RelentlessPersists beyond normal human limits.
Intelligence Ignis

Resonant Anchor: Ignis

Intelligence measures structured reasoning, analysis, and formal understanding. It reflects the ability to observe factual details, compare information, recognize patterns, and apply logic. Intelligence governs investigation, technical knowledge, strategic planning, and the diagnosis of observable systems.

When a character analyzes evidence, studies physical changes, or applies learned theory to understand a situation, they use Intelligence.

Intelligence understands through analysis.

Ladder — Increasing reliability in structured reasoning and pattern recognition
1LimitedStruggles to connect complex details.
2BasicUnderstands straightforward patterns.
3ReasonedApplies clear logic in most situations.
4 ✦SharpQuickly recognizes inconsistencies.
5BrilliantExceptional analytical insight.
6FormidableSees systems and consequences with extraordinary clarity.
Wisdom Vitae

Resonant Anchor: Vitae

Wisdom represents experiential awareness, judgment, and intuitive understanding. It reflects recognition born of lived experience, empathy, and instinct. Wisdom governs situational awareness, sensing danger, recognizing emotional truth, and discerning significance before formal analysis begins.

When a character feels that something is wrong, reads emotional shifts through experience, or recognizes meaning without visible proof, they use Wisdom.

Wisdom understands through experience.

Ladder — Increasing reliability in perceiving significance and responding with sound judgment
1NaïveFrequently misreads danger or meaning.
2UncertainNotices obvious signs but misses subtle significance.
3SoundGenerally aware of what matters.
4 ✦InsightfulQuickly recognizes shifts in mood or danger.
5SageDeep experiential understanding of people and circumstance.
6ProfoundRecognizes meaning before it becomes visible.
Social No Resonant Anchor

No Resonant Anchor — Social represents influence and leverage rather than elemental resonance.

Social measures interpersonal awareness and influence. It reflects how well a character understands power, emotion, and leverage within human interaction. Social governs persuasion, deception, intimidation, negotiation, and shaping outcomes through presence and communication.

Social influences what others believe and how they respond.

Ladder — Increasing effectiveness in social influence and navigation
1Off-PuttingStruggles to influence or connect.
2UneasyInconsistent presence or impact.
3PersonableCapable of ordinary persuasion.
4 ✦CompellingStrong presence and persuasive ability.
5MagneticNaturally influential.
6CommandingShapes the direction of a room.
