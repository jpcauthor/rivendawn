Season Months Days Notes
Season of Renewal INDOMITABLE, AERIS, WELLSPRING 84 Beginning of the yearly cycle; renewal and new beginnings
Season of Flow AQUA, RESONANCE, IGNIS 84 Movement, growth, and rising activity
Season of Vitality VITAE, CONCORD, TERRA 84 Height of life, strength, and productivity
Season of Echoes ECHO, OBTENEBRATE, HARMONY 84 Reflection, balance, and preparation for the year's close
Season of Vigil VIGIL 28 Final month marking the closing watch of the yearly cycle
Year Day — 1 A universal festival day occurring after Vigil 28. This day exists outside the calendar and week structure and marks the completion of the yearly cycle.
Leap Year — Day of Reconciliation — 1 Occurs every 4 years after Year Day. A solemn observance dedicated to forgiveness, settling grievances, renewing treaties, and restoring harmony before the new year begins.
