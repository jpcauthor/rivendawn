The Chosen

They arise from every corner of the realm—not by blood or by land, but by a mysterious touch of the Song that sets them apart from the common experience. Whether hailed as instruments of divine purpose or viewed with wary suspicion, the Chosen manifest insights and abilities that challenge the very boundaries of resonant theory.

Within the Citadels, their appearance attracts intense study, as they often uncover truths hidden deep within the Song. Their existence is a reminder that the relationship between individuals and the music of the world is far more complex than any single theory can describe.

Uncover the legends of those who walk a path defined by the Song itself.

Among the peoples recorded in the Citadel’s archives, few are spoken of with as much curiosity and debate as The Chosen. Unlike other cultural groups whose identities are tied to ancestry, Lands, or shared heritage, the Chosen are defined by a far more unusual distinction: each individual among them is believed to have been touched by the Song in a manner that sets them apart from ordinary experience.

Citadel scholars generally use the term Chosen to describe individuals whose lives appear to have been profoundly shaped by encounters with Resonance. These individuals often demonstrate unusual sensitivity to the Song, sometimes manifesting abilities or insights that exceed what is typically observed even among trained Resonant practitioners.

Unlike most peoples of Rivendawn, the Chosen do not form a single homeland or unified society. They arise from many different cultures and regions, appearing within communities that otherwise share little in common. For this reason, the Chosen are best understood not as a separate lineage, but as a distinct condition or calling recognized across many societies.

Historical records describe a wide range of responses to the presence of the Chosen. Some cultures regard them with reverence, believing them to be instruments through which the Song reveals its deeper purposes. Others treat them with caution, wary of the unpredictable consequences that sometimes accompany powerful Resonant ability.

Within the Citadels, the appearance of individuals believed to be Chosen has long attracted scholarly attention. Such individuals are often encouraged to study their abilities under guidance, both to better understand their connection to the Song and to ensure that their talents develop responsibly. In many cases, the archives contain detailed records of their achievements, discoveries, and influence upon the events of their time.

Despite the fascination they inspire, the Chosen rarely live apart from the societies in which they are born. Many continue to serve as soldiers, scholars, artisans, or travelers, their abilities simply adding another dimension to the roles they already fulfill. Others pursue paths of study or service that allow them to explore their connection to the Song more directly.

Because the Chosen appear across many cultures and generations, their presence has contributed to numerous legends throughout Rivendawn’s history. Some accounts credit them with turning the tide of great conflicts or uncovering truths hidden within the Song itself. Other stories portray them as wanderers whose lives follow paths difficult for ordinary people to understand.

For students of Rivendawn, the Chosen present an important reminder that the Song continues to shape the world in ways that cannot always be predicted or easily explained. Their existence suggests that the relationship between individuals and the Song is far more complex than any single theory has yet been able to fully describe.
