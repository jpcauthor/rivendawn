The Theory of Resonant Instruction

Concerning the Nature of the Song and the Limits of Its Instruction

I. On the Nature of the Song

Resonance is not conjuration. It is not prayer. It is not the summoning of distant power.

Resonance is the structured instruction of the Song — a pervasive lattice woven through all matter, motion, and breath within the world.

The Song does not think.

It does not interpret.

It does not judge.

It responds.

The Resonant does not cast. They instruct.

And the Song obeys — within law.

II. The Three Conditions of Resonant Action

All acts of Resonance are governed by three immutable conditions.

  1. Clarity of Instruction
  2. Magnitude of Disruption
  3. Cost of Sustenance

The Song answers structured will, not vague desire.

The greater the alteration imposed upon the world, the greater the resistance encountered.

The Wellspring is finite.

No instruction is without burden.

Where one of these fails, the act collapses.

III. Magnitude and Resistance

Resonant action occurs within tiers of disruption.

Standard

Localized alteration.

Single-target manipulation.

Immediate and contained expression.

Expanded

Multi-target or structurally significant alteration.

Greater resistance.

Greater cost.

Mass

Battlefield or environment-wide alteration.

Extreme resistance.

Extreme cost.

The Song resists upheaval.

To bend much is to pay much.

Within structured combat, magnitude tiers may be restricted by discipline, role, and harmonic bandwidth.

Note: Within structured combat, magnitude tiers and other Resonant expressions may be restricted by discipline, role, and harmonic bandwidth as defined in Structured Resonance in Combat.

IV. Defining a Cast

A Cast is a single resolved Resonant expression targeting one clear outcome.

A Cast cannot bundle multiple independent effects.

The declaration must identify:

  • specific intent
  • scope
  • target

If multiple discrete outcomes are desired, they require multiple Casts.

Resonance is applied, not stacked.

V. Capability & Target Number

All Resonant Casts require mechanical resolution using the unified subtractive model.

Capability = Primary Attribute + Ability/Knowledge + Path Rating

Target Number = Baseline − Capability

The player rolls 1d20.

If the result is equal to or greater than the Target Number, the Cast succeeds.

If the result is lower, the Cast fails.

Resolution is binary.

Magnitude, resistance, and interference modify the Target Number, not Capability.

VI. Codified Magnitude Tiers

Magnitude modifies difficulty, not cost.

Three tiers of scope are formally defined.

Standard Expression

Single target or contained effect.

No modifier to Target Number.

Expanded Scope

Multiple targets or larger structural alteration.

Target Number +3

Mass Scope

Battlefield scale or large environmental manipulation.

Target Number +9

Magnitude increases risk.

It does not increase cost.

VII. Resonance Cost

Resonance cost is flat per Cast.

True Resonant Casters

  • 5 RP on a successful Cast
  • 8 RP on a failed Cast

Hybrids

  • 7 RP on a successful Cast
  • 9 RP on a failed Cast

Failure costs more because harmonic invocation occurred without resolution.

Energy was engaged but not harmonized.

Cost does not scale with magnitude.

VIII. Failure & Harmonic Inversion

A failed Cast represents misalignment.

The intended effect does not occur.

Resonance is still expended according to failure cost.

No additional consequence applies.

Natural 1 — Harmonic Inversion

The declared effect manifests upon the caster instead of the intended target.

If the Cast was declared at Expanded or Mass scope, the backlash reflects that magnitude.

The Song does not punish.

It mirrors.

IX. Ultimate Success

A Natural 20 represents perfect harmonic resolution.

The Cast succeeds regardless of Target Number.

Magnitude modifiers still apply to the declared action.

A Natural 20 does not expand scope beyond what was declared.

Ultimate Success effects are defined within each Calling’s documentation.

Luck does not amplify.

It validates.

X. Harmonic Dissonance

Harmonic Dissonance represents sustained contradiction between a character’s declared Harmonics and repeated behavior without narrative transformation.

When Harmonic Dissonance is active, the Static Veil applies.

Static Veil

+3 to all Target Numbers for Ability and Knowledge checks, including Resonant Casts.

Dissonance increases difficulty.

It does not alter Capability or Baseline.

Harmonic Dissonance remains until the character demonstrates sustained realignment or formally redefines their Harmonic identity through narrative development.

Application of Harmonic Dissonance is at Watcher discretion.

XI. Creativity and Constraint

Resonance rewards knowledge.

Instruction must obey law.

Combustion requires combustible matter.

Pressure requires medium.

Mass must exist to be moved.

Life cannot be weaponized through Vitae.

The Song does not create impossibility.

It accelerates what is already possible.

XII. On Living Systems

Living beings resist deep alteration.

Resonance may:

  • strike exterior surfaces
  • constrain movement
  • agitate surface matter
  • reshape environment

Direct lethal alteration of internal structure requires physical interaction or lawful pathway.

Resonance may not instantly unmake internal structures or command life to undo itself.

The world protects its living patterns.

XIII. The Final Principle

Resonance is not dominion.

It is alignment.

It is structured will within immutable law.

Those who treat the Song as limitless will be corrected.

Those who treat it as law-bound may shape the world — but only as far as clarity, magnitude, and Wellspring allow.

The Song does not think.

It obeys.
