The mechanics of Rivendawn are built to serve a single purpose: to translate intent into consequence with clarity, consistency, and thematic cohesion. Across every layer of play—whether a character is attempting a mundane task, engaging in structured combat, invoking Resonance, or struggling to survive mortal injury—the system is designed to answer a single question: how does the world respond when a character acts?

Rather than scattering resolution across disconnected subsystems, Rivendawn uses a unified mechanical architecture. The core engine establishes how success and failure are determined, while supporting documents explain how that engine behaves during combat, environmental challenges, Resonant instruction, healing, and other situations that arise during play.

Within this section, each document contributes a specific piece to the overall mechanical framework:

  • Combat System — Defines the structure of combat encounters, including initiative, movement, engagement, and damage resolution.
  • Common Resolution Patterns — Standardized approaches for common gameplay situations such as opposed rolls, assistance, and environmental difficulty.
  • Dice Resolution System — The universal engine used to determine success or failure across all actions in the game.
  • Healing Quick Guide — A rapid reference for combat healing, stabilization, and survival during encounters.
  • Healing System — The complete rules governing injury, recovery, restorative healing, and Resonant Transference.
  • Resonant Path Effects — Describes the mechanical consequences produced when Resonant actions succeed.
  • Structured Resonance — Explains how Resonance functions under the strict rules of structured combat.
  • The Theory of Resonant Instruction — The philosophical and metaphysical laws that govern the Song and the instruction of Resonant power.

Together these documents form the complete mechanical architecture of Rivendawn. The Dice Resolution System provides the foundation, combat rules define the structure of conflict, healing rules govern survival, and the Resonant systems describe how the Song alters the world in response to a character’s will.

This page serves as a guide to the system as a whole. The documents themselves provide the detailed procedures, rules, and examples required to bring the mechanics of Rivendawn fully to life at the table.
