The Geography of Rivendawn

You want to understand the land. Good. Most people don't bother until it kills them.

Rivendawn is not a backdrop. It is not the quiet stage upon which interesting people do interesting things. The mountains do not care that you have somewhere to be. The Rivens do not pause for the unprepared. The north does not distinguish between the brave and the foolish — it simply takes them both with equal indifference.

What follows is what is known. The regions that have been settled, named, and argued over for centuries. The wounds in the world that have never healed. The wilderness that exists beyond every border anyone has ever drawn. And the things that live in all of it — most of which did not ask for your company and will not thank you for providing it.

Learn the land before you walk it. The land already knows you.

— The Watcher
Part I Rivendawn The shape of the world — an overview of its lands, its boundaries, and the maps that chart what civilization has managed to record.
Part II The Forge Lands Iron peaks, frozen passes, and cavern-riddled stone — the mountainous western territories that made endurance the first law of survival.
Part III The Cradle Lands Fertile riverlands, broad valleys, and the great water routes that carry trade, memory, and ambition through the heart of civilization.
Part IV The Limestone Lands Pale stone uplands, cavern-riddled hills, and winding ridges that divide the south into a landscape of hidden valleys and stubborn communities.
Part V The Thalmen Reach The vast frozen north — sweeping plains of ice and snow where the wind is the only constant and survival demands everything the land will teach you.
Part VI The Rivens Wounds in the fabric of the world — toxic, expanding, and hostile to all life. The land remembers every scar, and the scars are still growing.
Part VII The Land Itself Beyond the settled regions lie wilderness, frontier, and territory that no map has fully charted — the deeper world that does not wait to be discovered.
Appendix Bestiary The creatures of Rivendawn — what shares the land, what hunts in the dark, and what has been changed by things that should not be touched.
